# Community Introduction Template

Use this template when drafting posts to introduce a venture in a relevant online community.

## Format

```markdown
**Community:** {{COMMUNITY_NAME}} (e.g., r/freelance, Agency Slack group)
**Platform:** {{PLATFORM}} (Reddit, Slack, Discord, forum, etc.)
**Venture:** {{VENTURE_SLUG}}

---

**Title:** {{POST_TITLE}} (if applicable)

{{POST_CONTENT}}

---

**Community rules checked:** {{YES/NO}} (always check before posting)
**Self-promotion rules:** {{RULES_SUMMARY}} (what does this community allow?)
**Value to community:** {{VALUE}} (what does the community get from this post?)
```

## Guidelines

- **Lead with value, not promotion** — share an insight, ask a genuine question, contribute to a discussion
- **Be transparent** — if sharing your own product, say so. "I'm building X" not "Check out this amazing tool"
- **Follow community rules** — every community has self-promotion rules. Read and follow them.
- **Engage, don't broadcast** — respond to comments, answer questions, be a community member
- **One community at a time** — don't cross-post the same content everywhere simultaneously
- **Genuinely participate first** — if you're new to a community, contribute before promoting

## Submission

After drafting, submit to the Decisions DB via the `submit-for-approval` skill:
- Type: Outreach
- Include the full post and community name in Context
- Note the community's self-promotion rules
