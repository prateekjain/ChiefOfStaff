# Cold Email Template

Use this template when drafting outreach emails for approval.

## Format

```markdown
**To:** {{RECIPIENT_NAME}} ({{ROLE}} at {{COMPANY}})
**Email:** {{EMAIL_ADDRESS}}
**Subject:** {{SUBJECT_LINE}}
**Venture:** {{VENTURE_SLUG}}

---

{{EMAIL_BODY}}

---

**Why this person:** {{RELEVANCE}} (why they'd find this genuinely useful)
**Follow-up plan:** {{FOLLOW_UP}} (when to follow up if no response)
```

## Guidelines

- **Subject line:** Short (< 50 chars), specific, no clickbait
- **Opening:** Reference something specific about them or their work — show you did research
- **Body:** Keep under 150 words. One clear ask. Focus on their benefit, not your product.
- **CTA:** One specific, low-friction action (reply, check a link, 15-min call)
- **Tone:** Professional, warm, concise. Not salesy.
- **No spam:** Only email people who would genuinely benefit. No mass outreach.

## Submission

After drafting, submit to the Decisions DB via the `submit-for-approval` skill:
- Type: Outreach
- Include the full email in Context
- Note why this person is a good fit
