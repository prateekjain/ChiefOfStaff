## 2026-03-14: [Marketing] Never fabricate testimonials or social proof

**What happened**: Builder agent generated a landing page with fake testimonials from made-up people (fake names, fake companies, fake quotes). Founder caught it immediately.
**Why it happened**: The agent treated testimonials as standard landing page copy to generate, without considering that fabricated quotes from fictional people are dishonest.
**Lesson**: NEVER create fake testimonials, reviews, or social proof. If the product has no real users yet, use alternative sections: "Built for" audience cards, "How it works" details, or a simple waitlist counter. Only add testimonials when we have real quotes from real users with their permission.
**Applies to**: All ventures, all stages. This is a hard guardrail — no exceptions.
